Hello! I'm Jade Wu.

I'm a multidisciplinary creative working at the intersection of art, technology, and the built environment. My practice focuses on creating experiences, provoking thoughts, and driving positive social impact.

I currently produce immersive experiences at NEON. Previously, I worked as an exhibition catalyst at teamLab, a product designer at Cambly, and a researcher at metaLAB (at) Harvard & FU Berlin and Harvard Joint Center for Housing Studies. Earlier in my career, I planned and managed the expansion of metro system (MRT) in Singapore.

Outside of work, I enjoy photography, glass-blowing, and adventure sports.

Fun fact: I'm Singapore's first female certified unmanned aerial vehicle (UAV) pilot.
